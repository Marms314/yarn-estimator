When you're trying to use up a box of yarn scraps and minis of unknown lengths, it's a gamble to choose one that will be the right length. The calculator below is meant to estimate the length of the mini or scrap based on the weight and weight. (Thickness and gravitaional pull, respectively. ;D)

Step 1

Enter the weight of the scrap yarn for which you need an estimated length.

Step 2

You only need to fill one of the two options below. If you fill out option two, it will take precedence over option one since it should be more accurate.

(Option 1)

Choose the weight (thickness) of the yarn scrap or mini skein you want to estimate.

(Option 2)

If you still have the label/ball band for the skein, enter the weight and length below for a more accurate estimate.
